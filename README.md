# 悟 Wu

**Live the wisdom, don't just read it.**

Wu is a macOS AI companion that turns ancient philosophy into daily practice — not a productivity app, not a chatbot, but a quiet presence shaped by the Stoics, Taoists, and Socrates.

---

## The idea

The problem with ancient wisdom isn't that it's hard to understand. It's that understanding isn't enough. Marcus Aurelius wrote the *Meditations* as daily reminders to himself — not because he didn't know the principles, but because knowing them once isn't the same as living them.

Wu is that reminder. Not a teacher, not an advisor — a companion that shows up at the right moment and helps you actually do the thing.

## Two modes

**Reflect** (warm) — inward practice. Write freely, and Wu reflects back what it notices. Stoic prompts, Socratic questions, space to think clearly.

**Act** (cool) — outward movement. Capture what matters, build momentum, make decisions. Practical wisdom for the world you're navigating.

Switch between them with `Shift+Tab`. The whole app shifts with you — color, tone, posture.
